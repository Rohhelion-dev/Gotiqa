About Gotiqa Farm

Our Story

Gotiqa Farm was founded in 2026 with a vision to revolutionize goat farming in our region. What started as a small operation with just 20 goats has grown into a thriving agricultural enterprise with over 127 healthy animals and growing.

Our journey has been marked by a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and community development. We leverage modern technology and traditional farming wisdom to create a harmonious balance that benefits our animals, our customers, and our environment.

Today, Gotiqa Farm stands as a beacon of agricultural excellence, proving that smart farming combined with genuine care for animals can create a sustainable and profitable business model.
